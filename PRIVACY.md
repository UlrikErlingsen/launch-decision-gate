# Privacy

GateSignal is local-first. The app has no built-in account system, telemetry SDK, advertising, remote database, or required API connection. Data entered in the browser is processed by the local Streamlit process and remains in that process unless the user downloads or otherwise moves it.

Project uploads may contain confidential forecasts, customer research, supplier details, product claims, or personal names. Use GateSignal only on a device and network appropriate for that information. Treat exported `.xlsx`, `.json`, and `.zip` evidence packs as sensitive business files and apply your organization's retention, encryption, access-control, and deletion rules.

If someone hosts GateSignal, that operator becomes responsible for transport security, authentication, server logs, backups, access controls, retention, incident response, and applicable privacy obligations. The local-first defaults do not automatically make a hosted deployment private.
