# Code of conduct

Contributors are expected to communicate respectfully, critique ideas rather than people, acknowledge uncertainty, and make space for differing experience and expertise. Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, and abusive conduct are not accepted in project spaces.

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when needed to protect a constructive environment. Report conduct concerns privately to the project owner; reports will be handled with discretion and without retaliation.
